Wonder Ballroom Venu Information

The Wonder Ballroom is a beautifully restored historic building constructed in 1914. With vaulted ceilings and plenty of free parking, the Wonder Ballroom is a versatile facility for a wide variety of performances and events.

Floor Space

Approx. 50′ x 70′. Perfect for ballroom dancing.

Stage Dimensions

25 feet wide x 16-18 feet deep (proscenium with apron); fully curtained.

Venue Capacity

778 open floor (450 if chairs are used)

Sound

IN HOUSE:

  • Midas Pro 2 console (stage box located at FOH position)
  • 48 channel copper core splits with (3) 12 channel sub snakes
  • (4) Channels of Cat5 Ethercon Snake
  • L’Acoustics ARCS system: (4) boxes per side, (3) per side set up as mains and the inner box can be set up as in fills and can be shaded.
  • All ARCS are powered by Lab Gruppen power amplifiers and processed by BSS Mini Omnidrive (2 FP6400’s and 2 FP3400’s)
  • Subs are 2 sets of SB218’s, 2 per side.
  • Audio power: 100-amp service on camlocks

IN HOUSE MONITORS:

  • Avid SC 48
  • (8) Amplified mixes of Crown CE 4,000 for wedges
  • (8) Coaxial McCauley S15’s 15”
  • (1) 3-way tri amped drum fill, Renkus Heninz STX4/44 High and Renkus Heinz STX4L for the subwoofer.
